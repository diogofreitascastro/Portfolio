Christmas advent event

  • Category: Illustration
  • Format: 1920 x 1080p
  • Year: 2019

Part of a Christmas Advent project that the RuneScape's Art Community decided to host, I made this illustration for the 17th of December 2019. It features a well-known character in a cozy house, having his hot chocolate. It is wearing the most recent Christmas merchandising from the official game store, which I decided to throw in as a little bonus for those looking out for it. It was posted on Reddit and Twitter as part of this bigger project. On the final day, there was a big collaboration with all the art pieces. It was drawn in Photoshop.

The illustration

Guthix, at his cabin, with some hot chocolate milk.
