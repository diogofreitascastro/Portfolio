RuneScape Card Deck

  • UNIVERSITY:
  • CATEGORY: Web Design, Illustration
  • TEAM SIZE: 1
  • YEAR: 2020

This design was part of a competition from an online game: RuneScape. We were asked to create a pet/familiar. From all the categories, I picked one about a pet that changed based on the location that the player would be in. I decided to design a chameleon that would change colour and some of its shape to fit in with the environment of said region. After submitted, my design passed to the in-game polls where every player could vote, having finished third, very close to the second place. As a note for all the following illustrations, my name in game is “Merds”, hence the signature. It was drawn entirely in Photoshop.
